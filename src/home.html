Lewis Hamilton Equals Michael Schumacher's Record

Hamilton is now the most decorated racer the sport has ever seen - with more wins (94), more poles (97) and more podiums (163) than any driver who has gone before. The 35-year-old, who grew up on a Stevenage council estate, delivered what has been described as his best display of the season as he won a dramatic Turkish Grand Prix on Sunday morning.

Four-time world champion Sebastian Vettel will retire from Formula 1 at the end of the 2022 season.

The German, who made his debut with BMW Sauber in the 2007 United States Grand Prix, revealed the news via his newly created Instagram account – his first foray into social media. Vettel won all four of his championships with Red Bull between 2010 and 2013 and is third on the all-time list of Grand Prix winners with 53 victories.

AlphaTauri announce 2023 livery launch date – and confirm name of new car

AlphaTauri have announced they will be holding a launch event in New York City to reveal their new livery for the 2023 season. The team also confirmed the car will be called the AT04. The new car will be raced by Yuki Tsunoda, who will be competing in his third season in Formula 1, and rookie Nyck de Vries, who replaces the Alpine-bound Pierre Gasly.
