Who are we?

F1 corner is an independent website created by fans to bring formula one closer to the fans. Our main goal is to aid Formula One fans in navigating through the sport and it's schedules. And to offer timely news about the drivers as well as the teams.

We are impartial and independent. It is our aim to be as detailed and accurate as possible, so if you spot any errors or inaccuracies in our coverage, please let us know as we pledge to address mistakes as quickly as possible.
